Phase Two
ASSEMBLE TEAM
Each team independently purchases 3 Scripts, 3 Cast, and 3 Crew. Choose any mix of star tiers. Higher stars cost more but impress directors and boost film quality. Advertising budget carries over to production.
Phase Three
SIGN DIRECTORS
Offer each director a flat fee + % of their film's box office revenue. Directors may accept, counter, or decline based on the quality of your roster. Each team signs 2 directors — each director can helm up to 2 of your 3 films.

Phase Four
ASSEMBLE MOVIES
Assign your Director, Script, Cast, and Crew to each of 3 films. Set a Marketing budget and choose a release window. Quality dominates — a great film with no marketing beats a poor film with massive spend. Marketing provides a boost (up to +50%) that breaks ties and separates close competitors.
Phase Five
BOX OFFICE RESULTS
Revenue is split by release window. Films compete on Score = Quality× × (1 + Marketing Boost). Quality multiplier ranges from 0.3× (all ★1) to 3.8× (all ★5). Marketing adds up to +50% on top. Films share the pool proportionally by score.
